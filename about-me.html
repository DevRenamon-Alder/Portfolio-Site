About Me

Hello! My name is Amber R. and I'm a full-stack web-developer. When I'm not coding I like to play video games such as Fortnite and the Pokemon games, as well as watch tv shows like Yugioh or Invincible. At home I often spend time with my cat, Tommy, while coding on my laptop. When working I like to consider myself an artist, and the projects I work on to be my art. This helps me stay on top of things and stay passionate even when getting error message after error message, because I know it's all part of the process and that with all good art it takes time to get it perfect. Some projects I have in the works include a browser game simulating the card game garbage, a random character generator or two, a pokemon guessing game based on What's That Pokemon, and more!
